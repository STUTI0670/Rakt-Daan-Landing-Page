About Us

At Rakt Daan, we aim to connect donors and recipients in times of need. Our mission is to promote the values of humanity by enabling a nationwide network for blood donation camps and immediate support.

Why Donate?

Save Lives

One blood donation can save up to three lives. Be the reason someone survives.

Community Care

Help patients with chronic conditions by ensuring they have access to treatments.

Feel Good

Donating blood promotes your well-being and helps rejuvenate your body.
